Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose Fanavar Steel over imported products?

Fanavar Steel offers international quality standards with the advantage of local manufacturing. Unlike imports from Pakistan, Iran, or China, we provide direct factory access, comprehensive warranties, and after-sales support right here in Afghanistan. Plus, you save on international shipping costs and delays.

What are your payment terms?

We offer a transparent payment policy. A deposit is required to initiate production, with the balance due upon completion/delivery. We do not operate on a credit basis to maintain our high production standards and material quality.

How does the power situation affect delivery timelines?

We are transparent about delivery timelines. While we strive for speed, factors like national power availability can impact production schedules. We communicate realistic timelines upfront and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer warranties and after-sales support?

Yes! As a local manufacturer, we are the first in Afghanistan to offer comprehensive warranties and reliable after-sales service. If you need maintenance or parts, we are just a phone call away—no waiting for international support.

Can I visit the factory?

Absolutely. We have an open-door policy for our clients. We encourage you to visit our facility to see our modern machinery, meet our team, and witness the quality of our production firsthand.
