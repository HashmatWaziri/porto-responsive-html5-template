## NOTES FOR IMAGE GENERATION

1. **Brand Colors to Use:**
   - Primary: Steel Gray `#818589`
   - Secondary: Lemon Yellow `#FEFE22`
   - Supporting: Black `#000000` and White `#FFFFFF`

2. **Style Consistency:**
   - All images should follow modern marketing template aesthetics
   - Clean, professional compositions with ample white/negative space
   - Consistent lighting and color grading across all images
   - 3D isometric illustrations for concept images
   - Professional photography style for team and location images

3. **Afghan Context:**
   - Team portraits should represent Afghan professionals
   - Location images should reflect Afghanistan setting where appropriate
   - Respect cultural context while maintaining international professional standards

4. **Image Formats:**
   - PNG for illustrations with transparency
   - JPG for photographs and complex images
   - SVG for icons and decorative elements

5. **Recommended Image Sizes:**
   - Logo: 130x32px (PNG with transparency)
   - Concept images: 400-600px width (PNG)
   - Team portraits: 400x500px (JPG)
   - Background images: 1920x1080px minimum (JPG)
   - Project showcases: 800x600px (PNG or JPG)
   - Icons: 75x75px (SVG)
